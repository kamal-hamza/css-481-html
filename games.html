Game 1: Age of Empires II

The Ultimate Real-Time Strategy Experience

I've been playing Age of Empires II for over a decade, and it remains my favorite RTS (Real-Time Strategy) game of all time. I used to play this with my dad a lot.

"Wololo! The monk conversion sound that echoes through gaming history."

— Every Age of Empires II Player
Game Information
Developer
Ensemble Studios (originally), Forgotten Empires (Definitive Edition)
Release Date
September 30, 1999
Genre
Real-Time Strategy (RTS)
Latest Version
Age of Empires II: Definitive Edition (2019)
Why I Love This Game
  1. Deep strategic gameplay with multiple civilizations
  2. Active competitive scene and community
  3. Endless replayability with different strategies
  4. Regular updates and new content
Favorite Civilizations
  • Britons - Nostalgic
  • Turks - They have these bombard towers that obliterate everything
  • Indians - The Elephants (Special Unit) were just fun to attack with
  • Byzantines - Had a special unit with guns that was pretty OP if you got a bunch of them together (might have been the turks tho)
Game Modes I Play

My preferred game modes include:

  • 1v1 Matches (Against my Dad)
  • Custom Scenarios / VS AI

Personal Rating: 10 out of 10 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Game 2: Need for Speed Series

The Thrill of High-Speed Racing and Crashing :)

The Need for Speed franchise has delivered some of the most adrenaline-pumping and heart racing, "racing" experiences in gaming history.

My Favorite NFS Titles
Need for Speed: Most Wanted (2005)

The definitive NFS experience. The blacklist system and police chases were revolutionary. Also there was this ramp that if you got on it none of the cops would be able to get you. There was also a golf course where you could knock over golf carts.

Best Feature
Intense police pursuits with multiple heat levels
Memorable Moment
Defeating Razor and reclaiming the BMW M3 GTR
Need for Speed: Heat

The ultimate car customization game (Also just looks very good). I found a glitch to farm money and beat all my friends.

Best Feature
Deep car customization with body kits, vinyls, and performance parts
Favorite Car
Porsche 911 RSR (OP Car)
What Makes NFS Special
  1. Perfect balance between arcade and simulation racing
  2. Incredible car selection and customization
  3. Epic soundtracks that define each game (Underground)
  4. Thrilling police chase mechanics
Favorite Cars Across the Series
My Top 4 NFS Cars
Rank Car Game
1 BMW M3 GTR Most Wanted
2 Porsche 911 RSR Heat
3 Mazda RX-7 Underground
4 Lamborghini Murciélago Hot Pursuit

Series Rating: 10 out of 10 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Game 3: Plants vs Zombies Garden Warfare 2

The Most Fun Third-Person Shooter

Plants vs Zombies: Garden Warfare 2 is a really nostalgic and goofy multiplayer shooter that I've enjoyed for years.

Game Details
Developer
PopCap Games
Publisher
Electronic Arts (EA)
Release Date
February 23, 2016
Genre
Third-Person Shooter, Tower Defense
Platforms
PC, PlayStation 4, Xbox One
Why This Game Is Amazing
  1. Unique and creative character designs
  2. Unbalanced gameplay between Plants and Zombies teams
  3. Variety of game modes for different playstyles
  4. Regular events and content updates (when active)
My Favorite Characters
Plants Team
  • Citron - Movement Orange*

* My most played character with over 100 hours

Zombies Team

Progression System

The game features an engaging progression system:

  • Character leveling and promotion system
  • Sticker packs with cosmetics and variants (some variants are genuinly broken)
  • Backyard Battleground hub world
  • Quests and daily challenges
  • Character customization options
